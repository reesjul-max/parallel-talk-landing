Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal advice?

No. Parallel Talk Parent is a communication assistance tool, not legal advice. We don't interpret court orders or tell you what you "should" do legally. Always consult your attorney for legal guidance.

How does AI safety filtering work?

Our three-tier system checks your message before sending. Tier 1 blocks harmful content (threats, harassment). Tier 2 suggests neutral alternatives for inflammatory language. Tier 3 offers clarity improvements. You always decide what gets sent.

Are the exports really court-ready?

Yes. Messages are timestamped, immutable (can't be edited after sending), and exportable in professional PDF format with certification statements. However, admissibility is ultimately up to your court and attorney.

What if my co-parent won't use it?

Both parents need to use the platform to communicate through it. However, even if your co-parent declines, the waitlist signup helps us understand demand and may influence them to reconsider later.

How much will it cost?

We're finalizing pricing but expect it to be affordable for most families ($15-30/month per parent). Waitlist members will receive early-bird pricing.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, and your messages are only visible to you and your co-parent. We never share, sell, or use your data for anything other than providing the service.

How is this different from other co-parenting apps?

Other co-parenting platforms primarily focus on scheduling, expenses, and basic messaging. While some offer tone detection, messages can still be sent even if flagged as potentially problematic. Parallel Talk Parent focuses exclusively on communication quality—our AI actively helps rewrite messages for clarity and prevents harmful content from being sent. We complement other apps' logistics features by providing a dedicated space for better messaging.
